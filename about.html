About Steary

The premium glassmorphic streaming and developer platform.

Our Mission

Steary was engineered to bridge the gap between premium visual aesthetics and high-performance frontend architecture. Our goal is to provide a seamless, beautiful, and fluid environment for discovering entertainment, developer tools, and cutting-edge AI integrations.

What We Offer

  • Premium UI/UX: A modern, glassmorphic interface powered by vanilla CSS and GPU acceleration for ultra-smooth performance.
  • Developer Tools Hub: Curated collections of the best open-source and commercial development utilities.
  • AI Ecosystem: A central repository for exploring and interacting with state-of-the-art artificial intelligence models and applications.
  • Custom Projects: Exclusive applications, extensions, and interactive experiences designed by our team.

Our Technology

Unlike heavy, bloated frameworks, Steary relies on pure Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We utilize advanced CSS techniques like clamp() for fluid typography and CSS variables for instant theme switching. Our backend relies on Supabase for real-time data synchronization without compromising speed.

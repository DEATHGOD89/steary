Help Center & FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Steary.

How do I change the theme?

Steary supports both Light and Dark modes. Simply click the Sun/Moon icon in the top right navigation bar to toggle between themes. Your preference will be saved locally on your device.

How do I open projects?

Navigate to the "Projects", "Dev Tools", or "AI Hub" tabs and click on any card. This will open an interactive preview within our custom high-performance iframe wrapper, without leaving the site.

Why are my settings lost when I change devices?

Currently, Steary uses local browser storage to keep things fast and private. This means your profile settings and theme preferences are specific to the browser and device you are currently using.

How can I access the Admin Dashboard?

The Admin dashboard requires a valid authorization key. If you are a site administrator, navigate to /admin.html and enter your credentials.

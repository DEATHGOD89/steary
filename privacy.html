Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and respect your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Steary does not actively track, record, or harvest your personal information. Our platform is designed to run locally in your browser.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage (localStorage) to save non-sensitive settings such as your preferred theme (Light/Dark mode) and mock profile configurations. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly connect it to an external backend service.

3. Supabase Integration

Some of our dynamic content (like the project cards and navigation menus) is fetched from our public Supabase database. We do not require you to log in or create an account, meaning your read-only access is entirely anonymous.

4. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. Any cookies present are strictly functional and required for external integrations (such as embedded YouTube videos).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please reach out to us at our Contact Support page.
